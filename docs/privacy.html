ChloeVR — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

ChloeVR does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All application settings, media library metadata, playback history, favorites, and user preferences are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SharedPreferences).

Eye Tracking

If you enable eye tracking features, all gaze data is processed locally on your device in real-time. No eye tracking data is stored, transmitted, or shared with any party. Eye tracking is used solely to improve the viewing experience (autofocus convergence adjustment).

Network Access

ChloeVR accesses the network only when you explicitly use the streaming browser feature to connect to video streaming endpoints that you have configured. The app does not make any network requests otherwise. No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.

Third-Party Services

ChloeVR does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The DeoVR-compatible streaming API connects only to endpoints that you manually configure.

Children's Privacy

ChloeVR is a general-purpose video player application. It does not target children and does not collect data from any users regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
